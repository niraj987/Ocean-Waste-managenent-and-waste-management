Waste Management

We are committed to responsible waste management practices. Our approach focuses on the three R's: Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle.

Solid Waste

    Liquid Waste

    Our liquid waste management involves proper treatment and disposal to prevent contamination of water sources. We also promote water conservation practices.

    Gas Waste

    We strive to reduce greenhouse gas emissions through energy-efficient practices and the use of renewable energy sources. We also monitor and manage any gas waste produced in our operations.
